About Me

My name is Michael and I was born in Saint Catharines, a small town outside of Niagara Falls, in Canada. I have also lived in Michigan, and North Carolina in the past. By day, I am a business owner and Certified Residential Real Estate Appraiser with almost 15 years of experience.

By night, I am attending a bootcamp at SMU to become a full stack developer. I have an associates degree in Linux/UNIX and love the command line. I have always enjoyed tinkering with computer programs, websites, and applications and now I want to become a professional!

I am currently learning about HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap, JQuery, APIs, AJAX, Node.js, NPM, SQL, MySQL, MongoDB, Express.js and a whole bunch more...

I currently live outside of Dallas, in Mesquite Texas, with my beautiful wife Lindsay, who is a first-grade teacher. Lindsay and I have two dogs and two cats. I have two amazing adult children who live outside of Austin.

In my free time I enjoy spending time with friends and family, traveling to new places, learning new things, and meeting new people.
